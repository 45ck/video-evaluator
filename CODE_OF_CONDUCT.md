# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Treat contributors with respect and keep discussion technical, direct,
and grounded in evidence.

Good behavior here means:

- critique code and claims, not people
- prefer concrete artifacts and reproduction steps over vague opinions
- be explicit about capability limits
- avoid inflated claims about what the repo can currently do

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- bad-faith argument or repeated misrepresentation
- publishing private or sensitive artifacts without permission
- overstating results after being shown contradictory evidence

## Enforcement

Maintainer interpretation applies. The repo may remove comments, close
threads, or block contributors who repeatedly damage the signal quality
of the project.
